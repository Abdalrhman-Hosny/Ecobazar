About This Product

Our organic Chinese cabbage is carefully cultivated using sustainable farming practices that prioritize soil health and biodiversity. Each head is harvested at peak freshness to ensure optimal flavor and nutritional value.

Key Features:

  • Rich in vitamins K, C, and folate
  • Excellent source of dietary fiber
  • Low in calories, high in nutrients
  • Versatile for various culinary uses

Storage Instructions:

Store unwashed in the vegetable crisper drawer of your refrigerator. For best quality, use within 5-7 days of purchase. Can be blanched and frozen for longer storage.
